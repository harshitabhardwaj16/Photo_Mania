Instructions:


 please upload an image file under the "Choose File" button and then play with filters by selecting the filters under Select Filter section.


Filter Description:


Grey:  Filter your uploaded image by using contrasting color hues based only on white and black.
Red:  The reddish version of Grayscale filter. The base colors are Red and white.
Blur:  Make the uploaded picture blurring as well as opaque.
Rainbow:  Filter your uploaded image in a way that imitates the arrangement of sunlight spectrum.
Add Frame:  Add a photo frame to the uploaded picture.
Reset Image:  Reset the uploaded image to its original version.
